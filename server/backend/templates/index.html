FAQ

What is TrustlessCrowd.Network?

TrustlessCrowd.Network is a serverless crowdsourcing dApp on Polygon Blockchain. Unlike traditional web 2.0 crowdfunding platforms, there are no platform fees. You can create a crowdfunding campaign and get your project funded. Crowdloaning will be available soon.

What does trustless mean?

The trustless aspect of TrustlessCrowd.Network means that there is no immediate third party that is establishing the crowdsourcing process. Contract code and game theory guarantee trust. Campaign creators are required to deposit the collateral value of the amount that they are trying to raise. This collateral serves so that the creators are encouraged to ship their products to the buyers. Buyers pay a collateral value of the same amount that a product costs. Once buyers receive their physical reward, they then claim the reward. Claiming a product unlocks the collateral of the item back to the buyer. And campaign creators get the collateral for that reward, as well as the value of its price.

How is TrustlessCrowd.Network serverless?

TrustlessCrowd.Network is serverless because it serves as a single HTML file with inline CSS and javascript. Pinata.cloud hosts this HTML file on IPFS as well. You can reach this website on the Pinata service link. The HTML file is the front-end implementation that hooks to Polygon network. So there are no centralized servers involved.

How do I start a campaign?

You can start a campaign by visiting TrustlessCrowd.Network and click on the Create Campaign button. Then you need to fill out the form's information. Create an account on the pinata.cloud and create an API key and secret that you paste into appropriate fields. Watch this video for some additional help. Make sure to have USDT funds in the amount of collateral value that you are trying to raise.

Why do I need to deposit collateral when starting a campaign?

This collateral serves so that the creators are encouraged to ship their products to the buyers. Buyers pay a collateral value of the same amount that a product costs. Once buyers receive their physical reward, they then claim the reward. Claiming a product unlocks the collateral of the item back to the buyer. And campaign creators get the collateral for that reward, as well as the value of its price.

How do I buy a reward for a campaign?

You can buy an item on the right-side panel of the campaign's page by clicking the Buy button. Make sure you have USDT and enough funds to pay for the collateral amount.

Why do I pay collateral when I buy a reward?

A buyer must pay the collateral when buying a reward to be motivated to claim it once the physical item is delivered. Claiming the product releases the collateral back to the account of the buyer.

How and when do I get my reward shipped?

First, you need to deliver your contact information to the campaign owner by reaching out to their email. After that, they will ship their unit when they are ready. Once you get your item, claim the reward and release the collateral values.

How do I deliver my address information to the campaign creator?

Each campaign has the creator's email address on its page. Use that information to contact the campaign's owner.

How can I support TrustlessCrowd.Network?

You can support the TrustlessCrowd.Network developer by donating. There is a donate panel in the footer of the website.
